Chicken Curry

Ingredients(serves 4 people)

  • For the Chicken
    • 1 kilo of chicken
    • A bulb of onion
    • 2 cubes of maggi
    • Salt;to taste
    • Parsley
    • Ginger and Garlic
    • Fresh pepper
    • Thyme and curry
      • For the Curry sauce
        • Half a cabbage(sliced)
        • Red,green,and yellow bell peppers(diced)
        • A bulb of onion(diced)
        • Chicken stock
        • Curry
        • Turmeric
        • Black pepper
        • Cameroon pepper
        • Carrots(diced)
        • Plain flour or cornmeal(as a thickener)

Instructions

1.Firstly,marinade the chicken in all the ingredients listed above for about 3o minutes

2.Then, steam the chicken a little before adding water to boil.

3.When the chicken starts boiling, you add in your curry,tumeric,black pepper,onions,plain flour or cornmeal and chicken stock.

4.Once the sauce or broth has thickened,you add in your cabbage,bell pepper and carrots to boil for 10 minutes.

5.Enjoy your hot chicken curry served with white rice!
